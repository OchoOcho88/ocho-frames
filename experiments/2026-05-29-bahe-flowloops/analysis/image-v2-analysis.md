## Findings

1. **Product-in-use realism is the soft spot.** The loop is draped diagonally across her raised leg, decorative rather than in functional resistance use. gpt-image-2 chose elegant composition over a believable exercise position. To force real use, specify a concrete position ("loop stretched taut around both thighs during a seated abduction"), but that trades the calm editorial vibe for exercise-demo. Decide per asset.
2. **Casting skews conventional fitness-aesthetic** (lean, toned). Strategic flag for Sportif positioning, not a generation flaw. If Lucy leans inclusive ("vanity-to-sanity" per the SWOT), prompts should specify diverse/relatable casting. If aspirational-premium, this is on-brand. Parked until brand direction is known.

Strongest asset of the experiment so far. Confirms gpt-image-2 can carry a full lifestyle brand image (person + product + text) in one pass. The pipeline's static side is validated for both product-hero and lifestyle formats.
