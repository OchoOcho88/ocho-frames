## Head-to-head: v2 (draped) vs v3 (taut)
v3 wins for product storytelling. v2 was a pretty vibe but the loop looked decorative; v3 shows what the product does while looking just as premium. The explicit taut/in-use instruction is a clear, no-cost improvement.

## Learnings
1. **RECIPE: to depict real product use, name the exercise position and add "stretched taut, clearly under tension, not draped or loose."** Works without sacrificing the editorial aesthetic. Promote to the doc.
2. **Casting varies across generations.** v3 came out more diverse (curly hair, warmer skin) than v2 purely from variance. If Sportif wants consistent or specific representation, specify it explicitly; gpt-image-2 will not hold casting on its own.

Strongest asset of the experiment. Resolves the v2 product-in-use gap. The static side of the pipeline is fully validated across product hero, lifestyle, and functional-use formats.
