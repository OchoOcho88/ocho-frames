# Client Intake Questionnaire for [Client Name]

> **What this is:** A 12-question intake to help us build a brand kit and creative direction for [Client]. Takes about 30 minutes to complete thoughtfully (or less if they send voice memos, see below). Their answers will shape every piece of creative we produce going forward, so it's worth giving real thought rather than rushing.
>
> **How to send:** Copy everything below the `---` line into an email to the client. Adjust the greeting, dates, and signoff to your voice. Attach a calendar link if you want to schedule a follow-up call to walk through the answers together.

Once I have your answers, I'll do my own market analysis and put together a brand kit and creative direction we can review together. Aim to get this back to me by **[DATE]** so we stay on track.
