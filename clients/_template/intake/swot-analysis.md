## Research helper

> Below is a prompt you can paste into Claude (Cowork, web claude.ai, or Claude Code) to do web research that populates the Opportunities and Threats quadrants. Run this before doing your own synthesis. It surfaces market data you'd otherwise need to manually search for. Replace `[CLIENT NAME]`, `[CATEGORY]`, etc., before pasting.

```
I'm preparing a SWOT analysis for a client launch and need market research for the Opportunities and Threats quadrants.

Client: [CLIENT NAME]
Category: [e.g., fitness accessories, DTC]
Launch timing: [e.g., September 2026]
Target customer (rough): [1-2 sentences]

Research and return:

1. **Category dynamics:** what's growing or shrinking in this category? Recent trends, consumer behavior shifts, platform shifts that matter. Cite sources.

2. **Top 5-7 competitors:** who are they, what's their positioning, what's working or not working for each. Include both incumbents and recent insurgents. Cite sources.

3. **Underserved segments:** customer types or use cases that competitors aren't speaking to well. Be specific about who and why.

4. **Recent failures / cautionary tales:** brands that launched in this category and stumbled. What went wrong?

5. **Cultural / regulatory threats:** anything happening that could hurt a new launch in this category (legal, platform-policy, cultural backlash patterns, supply chain).

Return as a structured markdown report with clear sources. Don't speculate. If you don't have a source, say so.
```
