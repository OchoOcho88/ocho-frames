# Setting up Lauren's email (lauren@lucywayne.com.au)

> Plain-language guide for Hugo. Lucy already has Google Workspace on lucywayne.com.au, so this is the easy version: we just add Lauren as a new user. About 5 minutes.
> Voice rule: no em dashes, no en dashes.
> Created 2026-06-18.

## Before you start

- You need to be signed in as the **admin** for lucywayne.com.au. That is almost certainly Lucy's main account (the one that first set up the email). If you are not the admin, do this on Lucy's login, or ask Lucy to make you an admin first.
- Adding Lauren adds **one paid seat**, roughly **AUD $8 to $12 a month including GST** (depends on Lucy's plan). Lucy's card on file is charged automatically.

## Steps

1. Go to **admin.google.com** and sign in with the admin account (Lucy's).
2. In the left menu click **Directory**, then **Users**. (Or click "Add a user" if you see it on the home page.)
3. Click **Add new user** at the top.
4. Fill in:
   - First name: **Lauren**
   - Last name: (her surname)
   - Primary email: type **lauren** (the @lucywayne.com.au part is already there)
5. Password: choose **Create password**, let Google auto-generate one (or type a simple temporary one), and tick **"Ask for a password change at the next sign-in"** so Lauren picks her own.
6. Click **Add new user**. Done. Google shows the new login details.
7. Click **Copy** or **Email the instructions** and send Lauren her email address plus the temporary password.

## What Lauren does next

- She goes to **gmail.com**, signs in with **lauren@lucywayne.com.au** and the temporary password, and sets her own password.
- She can now send and receive email as lauren@lucywayne.com.au.

## Nice to do after (optional, 5 minutes)

- **Email signature:** in Gmail, click the gear icon, See all settings, scroll to Signature.
- **2-step verification** for security: go to myaccount.google.com, then Security.

## If you get stuck

- **No "Add new user" button, or it is greyed out:** you are not signed in as an admin. Use Lucy's admin login, or have Lucy add you as an admin (admin.google.com, then Account, then Admin roles).
- **A billing or "user limit" prompt:** Lucy's plan needs one more seat. Follow the prompt to add a seat, or go to Billing in admin.google.com.
- **It turns out there is no Workspace after all** (you cannot find admin.google.com or any lucy@lucywayne.com.au mailbox): tell me and I will give you the full from-scratch setup steps (sign up, verify the GoDaddy domain, connect email, then add Lauren).
