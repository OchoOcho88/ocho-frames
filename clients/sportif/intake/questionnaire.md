# Sportif Intake Questionnaire

> **What this is:** A 12-question intake to help us build a brand kit and creative direction for Sportif. Takes about 30 minutes to complete thoughtfully (or less if you send voice memos, see below). Their answers will shape every piece of creative we produce going forward, so it's worth giving real thought rather than rushing.
>
> **How to send:** Copy everything below the `---` line into an email to the client. Adjust the greeting, the date, and signoff to your voice. Attach a calendar link if you want to schedule a follow-up call to walk through the answers together.
>
> **Suggested deadline:** ~10 days from send date. Gives them a working week plus a weekend without being rushed.
