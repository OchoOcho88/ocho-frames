## Q1. The "why"

Originally I was going to launch an activewear clothing brand. Decided to begin with an affordable luxury Fitnesswear accessories brand instead.

I found that Activewear accessories in the stores were very masculine with the black and fluro colours and I wanted to bring a new look to the market.

## Q2. The one-liner

Affordable Luxury Fitnesswear accessories and activewear.

## Q3. The product

Booty Bands, Vegan Ankle Strap. The Booty Bands and Vegan Ankle Straps will come in a cotton pouch.

Then I will be moving into recycled fabric and organic materials for the activewear range.

Also my bands are custom colours and are not in stock colours that other brands sell.

## Q4. The competition

PE Nation, AJE, Your Reformer, Move Active, Avara Athletics, ANINE BING, Leelo Active and Kikiva.

## Q5. The customer

Healthy young lady 18-45 who enjoys early morning pilates classes, walks. I like to wear beautiful, sustainable activewear, matching shoes and accessories.

## Q6. Where they hang out

Instagram Reels, Tiktok and newsletter's.

## Q7. Tone

Luxury, affordable, community focused, Catchy Statements such as "Too fashionable not to WEAR!"

## Notes vs SWOT assumptions (flags)

- **Cultural lane points clearly at Pilates plus design-led femininity.** Q5 ("early morning pilates classes") and Q1 (feminine, colourful answer to a masculine black-and-fluro market) both point at the same lane. This is the lane to validate in Pass 4.
- **Channel mix is Instagram Reels + TikTok + newsletters.** Facebook is NOT named by Lucy, despite the questionnaire prompt mentioning it. Budget benchmarking (Pass 5) should weight IG + TikTok + email, not Facebook.
- **Two reference brands, both about the founder, not the product.** Anine Bing (neat, elevated personal account) and Kikiva (founder content, behind the scenes, community). The signal is founder-led and community-led content, which matches the Q7 tone "community focused."
- **Aspirational style refs (AJE, PE Nation, Anine Bing) are fashion, not fitness.** Treat these as style/tier references, not direct competitors. Direct competitors are Move Active, Your Reformer, Leelo Active, Avara Athletics, Kikiva.
- **Wholesale-first** changes the asset list: we also need retailer-facing assets (line sheet, lookbook), not only consumer social.
