## The read for Sportif

- **Booty bands like Sportif's are already sold by two established brands:** Move Active ($19.60 to $28, soft colours) and Your Reformer ($29, in blue, sage, mocha and cream). They are minor items in bigger ranges, but the 'colourful booty band' space is not empty. So Sportif's difference will likely come from more than colour: a distinctive custom palette, a focused accessory-first brand and premium feel, and the vegan and pouch story. Price anchor for Sportif's booty bands: about $29.
- **Two brands sell ankle straps:** Avara ($39, plain Milk/Latte/Mocha) and Kikiva ($29.99, in Pink, Baby Blue and Black). So a colourful strap already exists at Kikiva, but as one item in a big activewear range. Sportif's edge is custom colours nobody stocks, an accessory-first vegan brand and a premium pouch. Price guide for the vegan ankle strap: $30 to $39.
- **Even the luxury reference (AJE) puts colour on its grip socks ($39, including rose).** Colour on the accessory clearly sells, it is just not owned by anyone in bands and straps.
- **Sold-out signals:** Move Active's Mat Grip Towels (Espresso, Cool Pink) and the Workout Terry Towel (Almond) are sold out, a sign towels are in demand. See demand-signals.md.
- **Price anchors:** resistance bands $19.60 to $28, ankle straps $39, grip socks $9 to $39, towels $26 to $79, bags $120 to $179. Sportif's bands and strap should sit in the affordable-luxury accessory band, with the cotton pouch as the premium cue.
- **Wider market scan (spotted while browsing):** NikeSKIMS now sells a Pilates Grip Sock at $50 (premium validation of the category), and ODE sells a woven pouch at $69.99 (Sportif's pouch idea, proven as a product people buy). See the board's market-scan section.
