# Sold-out watch and demand signals

A running list of competitor products that are sold out. A sold-out item is a sign of real demand and a possible product opportunity for Sportif, especially when it is simple to make and fits the accessory-first, colour-led lane. Idea started by Hugo, 2026-06-16.

## Move Active (checked 2026-06-16, from direct product shots)

- **Mat Grip Towel - Espresso** ($79) - SOLD OUT
- **Mat Grip Towel - Cool Pink** ($79) - SOLD OUT (the Matcha colour is still in stock)
- **Workout Terry Towel - Almond** ($26) - SOLD OUT

**Read:** towels are selling out across two product lines. The mat grip towel ($79) is a premium, simple-to-make item and two of its three colours are gone. This points to a real opening for a colourful, design-led mat or grip towel as an easy, higher-margin addition for Sportif. Treat it as a phase 2 idea (after the September bands-and-strap launch), not a launch distraction.

**Also worth noting (not sold out, but important):** Move Active does sell fabric resistance bands ($19.60 to $28, in Cool Pink, Ivory, Matcha). This corrects the earlier assumption that no named competitor sells a band. The band is still a minor item in their range and the colours are limited, so Sportif's colour-led, accessory-first angle holds, but Move Active is now the most direct product rival.

## Anine Bing (checked 2026-06-16, from accessory shots)

## How to use this

When checking each competitor, note any "Sold out" labels here with the product and price. Over time this becomes a cheap, ongoing read on what the market wants more of, and a shortlist of low-risk products Sportif could add.
