## Tips

- gpt-image-2 tends to make products look pretty but passive. For an in-use shot, the phrase "stretched taut, clearly under tension" reliably shows the band working (tested in our format notes).
- Keep the pattern subtle and tonal for the affordable-luxury read; loud high-contrast prints drift toward the White Fox look Lucy rejected.
- If you want a clean cut-out for layouts, generate on a plain white surface and remove the background in a design tool (gpt-image-2 cannot export transparent backgrounds).
- Stay aesthetic and functional in any caption, no health claims (no "tones," "burns," "builds glutes").
