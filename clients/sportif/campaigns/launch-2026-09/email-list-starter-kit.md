# Sportif Email and List Starter Kit

> Everything to turn Lucy's empty signup into a list worth joining. Built on the locked strategy: Lucy is the differentiator, email is the owned asset (Brand Value Plan Lever 5), founding access to the 500-unit September drop is the hook.
> Voice: Anine Bing restraint plus Kikiva warmth, Lucy in first person. No health or performance claims. No em dashes, no en dashes.
> Created 2026-06-18.

---

## 1. The subscribe offer (the one reason to join)

**Founding access to the September launch, plus Lucy's edit.**

Only 500 of the first run exist, so being on the list means being first. That scarcity is the offer. We pair it with a small instant gift (Lucy's Morning Edit, a one-page guide) so there is a reward the second they sign up, not just a promise.

This list does double duty: it grows Lucy's personal audience AND it is the Sportif pre-launch waitlist.

---

## 2. Signup section copy (for the website)

Replace the current "Drop us a line" contact framing with a clear subscribe value-prop.

- **Headline:** Be first to Sportif.
- **Subhead:** Join the list for founding access to the September launch (only 500 of the first run), plus Lucy's edit on styling, slow mornings, and the brand coming together. Get her Morning Edit guide the moment you sign up.
- **Button:** Join the list
- **Checkbox microcopy:** Yes, send me Sportif updates, launch access, and Lucy's edit.
- **Reassurance line (small, under the button):** No spam, just the good bits. Unsubscribe any time.

## 4. The free gift (lead magnet copy)

Title: **The Morning Edit, by Lucy Wayne.** Delivered as a one-page PDF (see `lucys-morning-edit.pdf`). Full text lives in that file; it is a slow-morning lifestyle guide (light, gentle movement, walk and coffee, a styling tip, and the "keep what you use beautiful" idea behind Sportif). Deliberately lifestyle framed, no fitness-result claims.

Why this one: Lucy is a stylist, so a styling-and-ritual guide is genuinely her expertise and on-brand, and it sets up the product (the things you reach for every morning) without selling hard.

---

## 5. Mini list plan (the whole flow on one page)

**The flow:**

1. Someone joins on the site, or via the Instagram link in bio.
2. GoDaddy sends its double opt-in confirm (fine to keep for now).
3. They get the Welcome email above, with the Morning Edit gift.
4. They enter the ongoing cadence below.

**Cadence, now to September (the slow ramp Lucy wants, 1 to 2 emails a month):**

- **June and July:** behind-the-scenes of building the brand, the first 500 bands arriving (early July), Lucy's styling edit. Warm and personal.
- **July and August:** a founding colourway sneak peek (let subscribers feel part of it), an ambassador or instructor feature, a styling note. Start a light "not long now" build.
- **September (launch):** announce the drop, a "first access for the list" early window before public, then a "live now" send and a gentle "almost gone" once stock moves. The list is the warm audience that makes launch day land.

**Practical, so we do not waste effort:**

- Collect in GoDaddy for now. Do NOT build elaborate automations there.
- When Shopify and Klaviyo are set up, export the GoDaddy subscribers to a CSV and import them to Klaviyo, then rebuild this welcome and cadence as proper Klaviyo flows. That is where the list should live long term (Brand Value Plan Lever 5).
- Keep one offer, one gift, one clear voice. Resist adding pop-ups and noise.
