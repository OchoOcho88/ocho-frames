> How the brand becomes a business: one hub everything feeds, and the path from now to September.

## How it all connects
*One hub, with Lucy as the face. Ambassadors and a little paid bring people in. The store and the email list are where we keep them.*

{{DIAGRAM}}

- People find Sportif through Lucy and ambassador content, tagged products in Reels, and a little early paid reach.
- One tap, on the link in bio or a tagged product, takes them to the shop.
- The shop turns a visit into a sale and an email sign-up, so even people who do not buy today become an audience we own.
- Happy customers and ambassadors send the next people back to the top, and it loops.
- Wholesale runs alongside: Lucy's gyms, in person, for faster near-term revenue and credibility.

## The Shopify store, the hub
*The store is the platform the whole plan runs on, the home every link, post and email points back to.*

- One hub at sportifcollection.com.au that looks and sounds like Sportif, so the brand stays consistent everywhere.
- Product pages carry the brand, not just the product: Lucy's story, the warm styling, the band in use, and the gift pouch offered at checkout.
- The email list lives here (Klaviyo on Shopify). Even the pre-launch "coming soon" page collects sign-ups, the welcome email greets them, and that owned list sells the next colour and the strap.
- One store, two markets: Shopify Markets runs Australia now and the US next year, so growing does not mean rebuilding.
- The first 500 sell as a founding drop, and when stock runs low the page switches to a back-in-stock waitlist, so a sellout grows the list instead of losing the customer.

## The plan, phase by phase
- **Now (this month):** open the store and a "coming soon" page that collects emails, lock the prices, pouch threshold and material, align the accounts to one look, build the ambassador shortlist, and start the email waitlist with Lucy's contacts.
- **Build (to July):** batch-film Lucy's content and the unboxing when the bands land, seed bands with instructors and micro-creators (our main reach), turn on a little paid for follows and signups, build the wholesale kit and start gym outreach, and connect the tracking.
- **Lead-up (August):** double down on what is working, tag products in Reels, run pre-launch sneak peeks and first access for the list, line up a couple of podcasts, and decide the batch 2 reorder early (the restock takes about 35 days).
- **Launch (September):** a Bondi or Double Bay event with local press, sell the 500 as a limited founding drop with a back-in-stock waitlist, run the email launch sequence, and switch on gym placements.
- **After launch:** turn on reviews and customer photos, add repeat-purchase and win-back emails, and consider a community group and TikTok Shop when it lands in Australia.

## What we need from you
- Open the Shopify account and give us access to build it.
- Lock the prices and the gift-pouch threshold (about $70), and choose the material (recycled, organic, or hemp).
- A comfort level for a little early paid spend, for follows and email in the build phase, not only at launch.
- A call on ordering batch 2 early, so a sellout does not leave five quiet weeks.
- Chase the trademark clearance; we will hold anything printed until it clears.
