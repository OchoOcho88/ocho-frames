## Why we are doing each (same order)

**Do now**

1. **Open Shopify and send the info:** nothing can be built until the account exists.
2. **Lock prices and the pouch threshold:** the store and any ads need final prices.
3. **Decide the material:** needed for the product listings and the sustainability story.
4. **Coming-soon page:** start collecting emails now, the band ships early July with nowhere to sell yet.
5. **Ambassador shortlist:** this is our main way to reach people, and delivery day is the moment to start gifting bands.

**Do next**

6. **Align the accounts:** a business profile unlocks product tags, and consistency makes it look like a real brand.
7. **Content rhythm:** the slow build starts now, and filming in batches means a busy week never goes quiet.
8. **Wholesale kit and gyms:** the fastest, warmest revenue while the audience builds.
9. **Customer comms:** a test inquiry went unanswered for 7 days, so we cannot rely on manual replies.
10. **Batch 2 timing:** the restock takes 35 days, so we plan it before we sell out.

**Then**

11. **Tracking:** so any ad spend is measurable.
12. **Small early paid:** organic from about 900 followers is too slow on its own.
13. **Podcasts:** borrowed audiences and credibility.
14. **Trademark:** protect the brand before committing anything to print.
