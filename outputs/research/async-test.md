# Maximum Documented Video Duration for ByteDance Seedance 1.0 Pro: A Technical Synthesis

Across official and integrator-facing documentation, the **maximum documented video duration for the ByteDance Seedance 1.0 Pro model is 10 seconds per generated clip**, with support for 5-second and 10-second fixed-length outputs at up to 1080p
