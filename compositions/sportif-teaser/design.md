# Sportif design system (for video compositions)

Source of truth: `clients/sportif/brand.md`. Do not invent colours or fonts.

| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| Blush Peach (primary) | #F0CDB3 |
| Caramel accent | #C6926E |
| Terracotta accent | #833827 |
| Chocolate anchor | #2D1814 |
| Cream ground | #F6EEE5 |
| Warm Charcoal type | #4A433C |

## Typography

- Wordmark and headlines: Glacial Indifference (local woff2 in `fonts/`), letter-spaced generously (0.2 em to 0.3 em for wordmarks).
- The SPORTIF wordmark is always letter-spaced caps with a short underline rule beneath.

## Motion feel

Calm, editorial, unhurried. Soft eases (power2/power3), slow drifts, gentle Ken Burns on photography. No bounces, no spins, no flashy wipes.

## What NOT to do

- No em dashes or en dashes in any on-screen text.
- No health or performance claims ("tones", "burns", "boosts").
- No hype register ("obsessed", "must-have", urgency).
- No black-and-fluro gym aesthetics, no harsh pure black backgrounds.
- No sustainability or material claims (fabric not locked yet).
