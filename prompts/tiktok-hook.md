# TikTok / Reels / Shorts Hook Video

## When to use
9:16 vertical, fast-paced, captions-first, AI voiceover. Built to win the first 3 seconds.

## Prompt

```
Using /hyperframes and /hyperframes-media, create a 9:16 TikTok-style hook video about [topic].

Length: [15 / 20 / 30] seconds
Resolution: 1080x1920
FPS: 30

Script (write it for me, then build):
- Hook (0–2s): a contrarian or surprising statement that stops the scroll
- Body (2s–end-3s): 2–3 quick points, one per scene, each ~5s
- Payoff (last 3s): the "so what" — what should they do or remember

Production notes:
- Voice: HyperFrames TTS (Kokoro), voice "af_bella" (or pick a voice that matches the energy)
- Captions: HUGE, bouncy entry per word, positioned in the upper middle of the screen (leaves room for TikTok UI)
- Caption style: yellow text on a dark drop shadow for max readability
- Background: dynamic — use stock b-roll from assets/video/ if available, or solid gradient with floating brand-color particles
- Music: low-volume background music that ducks under the voice
- Brand: pull from brand/brand-kit.md but lean toward MORE saturated, MORE bold (TikTok needs contrast)

Topic: [your topic]
Key claim or insight: [what makes this scroll-stopping]
Target viewer: [who you're talking to]

Output to my-projects/[project-name]/
```

## Notes
- The hook is everything. Spend 30% of your prompt energy on getting it sharp.
- Captions in the upper middle, not the bottom — TikTok's UI overlays the bottom
- Music is optional but typically helps retention. Keep it -18dB or quieter under voice.
